Information on the coronavirus

Against the background of current developments in connection with the
spread of the coronavirus, we have compiled the most important informations below.
Basically we recommend the following steps for the fastest possible assistance:

1. take note of the current travel recommendations of the Federal Office of Public Health (FOPH) and travel restrictions and advice  from the Federal Department of Foreign Affairs (FDFA) .

2. contact your tour operator or the airline  Whether and to what extent a free cancellation or rebooking is possible can change continuously.

3. if the providers do not cover any costs, check your insurance documents.
If you have taken out an individual – or annual travel insurance (Travel Protection and Secure Trip) since November 19, 2019, you will benefit from the expanded ser-vices of the new GTC from August 26, 2020.
Overview of the new GTC

If you have another travel insurance through a third party of ours, then the coverage for the termination or cancellation depends on the respective insurance. As a rule, epidemics and pandemics are excluded. With our experience as a travel insurer, we can advise these insured persons in order to find a suitable solution. However, we cannot assume any costs.

Since August 26, 2020 we cover the illness of an epidemic or a pandemic illness such as B. Coronavirus in all newly concluded short trip and annual travel insurance.

The extended benefits also apply to all short-term and annual travel insurances (travel insurance package & Secure Trip) that have been taken out since November 19, 2019 (for claims from August 26, 2020). Examples of insured events in connection with Coronavirus (see details in the GTC).

Are Allianz Partners customers affected by the coronavirus outbreak

Allianz Partners is the world's leading travel insurance expert. It goes without saying that we offer active assistance to travelers in need - regardless of insurance coverage.

For example, Swiss insured persons can access constantly updated medical information and other medical concerns regarding the Coronovirus from Medi24 , regardless of their location.

In the context of an epidemic situation (COVID-19), I no longer feel like leaving because I am afraid to travel. Can I cancel my trip and get a refund?

NO - Travel insurance does not cover the fear of travelling.

In the context of an epidemic situation (COVID-19), the country I was supposed to visit closed its borders. Can I cancel my trip and get a refund?

NO - This is not a insured event under your insurance policy. Please contact the relevant service provider.

After returning to Switzerland, I would have to go into quarantine due to the regulations of the FOPH, as my travel destination is classified as a risk country. I would therefore like to cancel my trip. Will the travel insurance cover any cancellation costs?

NO - General quarantine obligations due to official orders are not insured.

My trip is cancelled by the service provider, what should I do to change my travel insurance policy?

To change your individual insurance policy, please contact our service center on +41 44 283 32 22 from Monday to Friday from 8:00 to 18:00 or by e-mail to info.ch@allianz.com . We will then make the necessary adjustments. Annual insurance policies are subject to the general conditions of change.

Due to a recent contact with a COVID-19 infected person, I have been quarantined by official order. Can I cancel my trip and get a refund?

YES - You can cancel your trip. Compensation will be paid in accordance with the conditions and within the cover specified in your insurance policy.

How? By filing your claim on our online declaration site . You will be asked for supporting documents.

My travelling companion was placed in quarantine before we left on our trip for suspicion of infection with a virus such as COVID-19. Can I cancel my trip and get a refund?

YES - You can cancel your trip. Compensation will be paid in accordance with the conditions and within the cover specified in your insurance policy.

How? By filing your claim on our online declaration site . You will be asked for supporting documents.

During my trip I developed COVID-19 symptoms. Can I seek the advice of a doctor?

YES - Thanks to our tele-consultation you can have a consultation with a doctor by video or telephone 24 hours a day.

Due to an individually ordered quarantine (contact with COVID-19 infected persons) or a COVID-19 illness, I could not take my return flight. Does my insurance policy cover the cost of buying a new return ticket?

YES - We will reimburse you for the cost of purchasing a new return ticket, provided there is no travel warning for your destination. Please contact our emergency call centre in advance on +41 44 202 00 00.

During my trip, compulsory confinement is decreed by the competent local authorities. Can I be repatriated?

NO - General quarantine obligations due to official orders are not insured.

If I get into individual quarantine shortly before my return due to a positive COVID-19 test and have to extend my stay, will my additional costs (accommodation, food...) be covered?

YES - We will reimburse you for the additional costs (up to max. CHF 1500.-) in accordance with the GTC that arise due to the extension of the trip in the event of quarantine. Please contact our emergency call centre in advance on +41 44 202 00 00.
